Project Type

A sketch-style 2D puzzle-platformer prototype inspired by the sketchbook level from Split Fiction

A Game Jam project created by a team of three. Focused on creativity, narrative structure, and puzzle-based gameplay.

Note: the playable prototype is available in Russian only.

Creative Context

The story follows a hero who finds themselves inside an abandoned, unfinished literary world. Its plot has fallen apart, and the characters who once lived there have become silent silhouettes, trapped between rough sketches and forgotten ideas.

The hero's goal is to restore the missing fragments of the story, bring meaning back to the world, and inspire the writer to finish their work.

On a deeper level, the game reflects the role of a creative game development team in bringing fictional worlds to life through writing, storytelling, localization, and imagination.

My Contribution
