About

I have spent the last 5+ years in the game industry working in localization and workflow automation. Somewhere along the way, solving workflow problems pulled me deeper into the technical side of development. What started with spreadsheet formulas turned into JavaScript and Google Apps Script tools for automating internal production pipelines in gamedev.

But outside of my day job, I kept drifting toward making games myself. I have built 4 Unity projects through game jams, a hackathon, and I am currently developing an endless runner for Yandex Games with my friend.

I am especially drawn to RPGs, souls-like games, interactive storytelling, and games where player choices actually mean something. Gameplay scripting feels like the natural next step.
