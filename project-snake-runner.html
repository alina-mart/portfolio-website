Creative Context

This is a mini-game created for the anniversary of Vikings: War of Clans. The player controls a small Viking running through a changing sequence of environments inspired by the game's regions: Midgard, Jotunheim, Helheim, and Alfheim. The regions appear in the same order as they were introduced in the game, while milestone moments mark different years of the project's development and the release of new features.

The goal was to create a simple and accessible mini-game that would remind players how Vikings: War of Clans evolved over time, while also giving them a score-based challenge to replay and compete with.
