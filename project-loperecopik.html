Project Type

Internal project made as a New Year present for the team

My first Unity project, made solo in one month from scratch. Filled with local memes and inside jokes about the department and work in general.

Creative Context

This little simple game means a lot to me.

I had always wanted to get closer to game development, but for a long time it felt like something distant, complicated, and almost impossible to approach. After several years of working in the game industry, I suddenly asked myself: "What if I tried to make a cute and funny little game for my department, filled with our local jokes and shared memories?"

At first, the idea felt completely crazy. But the problem is, I love challenges. And I love crazy ideas.

So I opened Unity for the first time in my life and, in just one month, created a simple, cheerful, short platformer. It was made especially for my colleagues, and somehow this small project managed to make them cry from warmth and tenderness. It also helped bring our team even closer together.

This project will always have a special place in my heart. It was the moment when I realized that almost anything is possible. All you really need is enthusiasm, motivation, and a genuine desire to make something great.

My Contribution

  • Designed the game concept, story, level flow, and core gameplay structure.
  • Implemented 2D platformer gameplay in Unity, including player movement, scenes, collectibles, and interactions.
  • Used tutorials and AI tools to write multiple C# scripts.
  • Built levels around internal jokes, department references, and comedic gameplay moments.
  • Created narrative content, recorded voice-over assets, and produced a parody cover inspired by "The Dragonborn Comes" from Skyrim.
  • Delivered a playable internal build as a New Year holiday gift for the team.
